Our Dream Project — Business Plans

Plan 1: Online Agency

  • Service: Social media management, copywriting, or virtual assistance for US/global clients.
  • Target: Small businesses, startups, or entrepreneurs.
  • Startup Cost: $100–$200 for website, branding, and marketing tools.
  • Growth Potential: Scale with subcontractors once demand increases.

Plan 2: E-Commerce Store

  • Model: Dropshipping or print-on-demand.
  • Target Market: US and worldwide (niche products with high demand).
  • Startup Cost: $200–$300 for store setup, ads, and product research.
  • Growth Potential: Expand to own branded products once sales grow.

Plan 3: Lead Generation Business

  • Service: Finding and delivering high-quality leads to businesses in specific industries.
  • Target Market: Real estate agents, home service providers, and B2B companies.
  • Startup Cost: $50–$150 for tools and CRM.
  • Growth Potential: Charge per lead or on a monthly retainer basis.
